DAY 2 Exploring Hwange National Park

After a hearty buffet breakfast and enjoying the African morning, we get into 4x4 vehicles and begin our journey deep into the national park. Along the way, you will see huge herds of elephants, cheetahs hunting for zebras, rhinos yawning lazily in the hot African sun, and many other animals.

One of the main advantages of Hwange National Park is its lack of crowds. Even during peak months, the park never gets crowded. You will feel unity with nature and find yourself the only observer of the life of wild animals for many kilometers. It's an amazing feeling!

We will end this day by birdwatching. Birdwatchers will be impressed with the 500 species recorded here. Watching hundreds of birds flying in the sunset lights is even better than meditation. After sunset, we will return to our lodge to rest and recharge before the last day of our adventures. A hot dinner and unlimited drinks will be waiting for you.

In Hwange National Park, you can meet the Big Five and 100 other species of mammals that live in grasslands and woodlands.

Buffet breakfasts and dinners with unlimited drinks are included in the price. Lunches and picnics can be arranged for an extra price.

The best time is during dry winter months (from July to October). You will get many impressions from watching the animals coming to the watering place.

Our partner is Hwange Golden Lodge. It's a comfortable lodge with spacious rooms, comfortable beds, air conditioning, and other amenities.
