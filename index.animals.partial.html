Animals you'll meet

You will see many exotic animals in their natural habitat.

Rhino

The rhinoceros is the largest land animal after the elephant. Modern rhinos reach a length of 2-5 meters and weigh from 1 to 3.6 tons.

Best places to see:

  • Hluhluwe–Imfolozi Park
  • Kruger National Park

Cheetah

The cheetah is the fastest animal in the world: when chasing prey, it can reach speeds of up to 112 kilometers per hour.

Best places to see:

  • Masai Mara National Park
  • Amboseli National Park

Elephant

Elephants are giants among animals. The weight of the elephant is from 3 to 7 tons. Elephants in Africa often weigh up to 10-12 tons.

Best places to see:

  • Chobe National Park
  • Kruger National Park

Zebra

Zebras reach more than 2 meters in length, the weight is 300-350 kg. The zebra has poor eyesight, but the sense of smell is well developed.

Best places to see:

  • Hluhluwe–Imfolozi Park
  • Etosha National Park
