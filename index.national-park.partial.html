Hwange National Park is located in Zimbabwe’s northwest and is considered one of Africa’s top national parks. It should definitely be on any safari lover’s bucket list. Here you can meet the Big Five and 100 other species of mammals that live in grasslands and woodlands. The Belgium-sized park is home to 50 000 elephants. Our guides will show you cheetahs, leopards, lions, endangered wild dogs, and rare species such as roan and sable. We will take you to the most beautiful places and show African wildlife as it is.
